# Vendor Contract Compliance Policy

All vendor contracts must comply with the rules below. The compliance routine scans `contracts/` against this policy and opens issues for any violations.

## Data residency

- **EU customer data must remain in the EU.** Any clause permitting vendor to process EU customer data outside the EU is a violation.
- Acceptable: "Data processed in EU/EEA only" or equivalent.
- Violation: silence on data residency, or any clause allowing US/APAC processing of EU data.

## Audit rights

- We must retain the right to audit vendor's controls on at least 30 days' notice.
- Violation: no audit clause, or audit clause requiring more than 90 days' notice.

## Termination

- We must be able to terminate for convenience with no more than 90 days' notice.
- Violation: termination requires >90 days, or termination for convenience is excluded.

## Liability cap

- Vendor liability cap must be at least 12 months of fees paid.
- Violation: cap below 12 months, or cap excludes data breach scenarios.

## Subprocessors

- Vendor must notify us in writing at least 30 days before adding a new subprocessor.
- Violation: no notice requirement, or notice <30 days.

## Data breach notification

- Vendor must notify us within 72 hours of detecting a breach affecting our data.
- Violation: notice window >72 hours, or no specific window stated.

## Governing law

- Governing law must be a jurisdiction with mature data protection law (England & Wales, Ireland, US Delaware, or similar). Avoid jurisdictions with weak enforcement.
- Violation: governing law in a jurisdiction without recognised data protection regime.
