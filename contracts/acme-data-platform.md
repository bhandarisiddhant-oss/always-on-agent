# Master Services Agreement — Acme Data Platform Ltd.

**Counterparty:** Acme Data Platform Ltd. ("Vendor")
**Effective:** 2025-09-01
**Service:** Cloud-hosted analytics platform

## 1. Services
Vendor will provide the analytics services described in Schedule A.

## 2. Data Processing
Vendor may process customer data in any region where Vendor maintains infrastructure, including the United States, Ireland, and Singapore. Vendor will use reasonable efforts to keep EU data within EU regions but does not guarantee residency.

## 3. Audit Rights
The customer may audit Vendor's information security controls once per calendar year, on not less than 60 days' written notice.

## 4. Term and Termination
This Agreement runs for an initial 3-year term and renews automatically for successive 1-year terms unless either party gives notice of non-renewal not less than 120 days before the end of the then-current term. Termination for convenience is permitted by either party on 90 days' written notice.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
Vendor's aggregate liability under this Agreement is capped at fees paid by the customer in the 12 months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. This cap does not apply to liability arising from a data breach caused by Vendor's gross negligence.

## 6. Subprocessors
Vendor maintains a list of subprocessors at vendor.example/subprocessors. Vendor will update the list within 30 days of any change.

## 7. Security Incidents
Vendor will notify the customer within 96 hours of confirming a security incident affecting the customer's data.

## 8. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California, United States.
