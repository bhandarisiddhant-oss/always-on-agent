# Service Agreement — Globex Messaging Inc.

**Counterparty:** Globex Messaging Inc.
**Effective:** 2026-01-15
**Service:** Transactional SMS and email API

## 1. Services
Globex will provide SMS and email APIs as described in the Order Form.

## 2. Data Processing
All customer data will be processed exclusively within the European Economic Area (EEA). Globex will not transfer customer data outside the EEA without the customer's prior written consent.

## 3. Audit Rights
The customer may request an audit of Globex's security controls on 30 days' written notice, not more than twice per calendar year.

## 4. Term
This Agreement runs for 12 months. The customer may terminate for convenience on 60 days' written notice. Globex may terminate for material breach after 30 days' cure period.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
Globex's aggregate liability is capped at the fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim. The cap does not apply to liability for data breach, gross negligence, or wilful misconduct.

## 6. Subprocessors
Globex will provide the customer with at least 30 days' written notice before engaging any new subprocessor. The customer may object to a new subprocessor within 15 days, in which case Globex will either propose an alternative or the customer may terminate the Agreement.

## 7. Security Incidents
Globex will notify the customer within 24 hours of detecting a confirmed security incident affecting the customer's data.

## 8. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
