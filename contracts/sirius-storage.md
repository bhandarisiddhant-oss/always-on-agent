# Master Subscription Agreement — Sirius Storage SDN BHD

**Counterparty:** Sirius Storage SDN BHD
**Effective:** 2025-11-12
**Service:** Object storage and archival

## 1. Services
Sirius provides object storage, retention, and archival services.

## 2. Data Processing
Customer data is stored in Sirius's data centres in Malaysia and Singapore. Sirius may relocate data between its facilities at its sole discretion for operational reasons.

## 3. Audit Rights
The customer may, on not less than 180 days' notice and not more than once every two years, conduct an audit of Sirius's security controls. All audit costs are borne by the customer.

## 4. Termination
This Agreement has an initial term of 5 years and renews automatically for successive 5-year terms unless either party gives notice of non-renewal at least 365 days before the end of the then-current term. Termination for convenience is not permitted during the initial term.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
Sirius's aggregate liability under this Agreement is capped at three (3) months of fees paid. This cap applies to all claims, including those arising from data breach or loss.

## 6. Subprocessors
Sirius may engage any subprocessor it deems appropriate without prior notice to or consent from the customer.

## 7. Security Incidents
Sirius will notify the customer of confirmed security incidents in due course, taking into account the nature and circumstances of the incident.

## 8. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of Malaysia.
